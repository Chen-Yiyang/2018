Intro

Hello world! Welcome to the inaugural high school computing conference organised by students for O-level and A-level Computing students, started in 2017 and now into its second and bigger year with more participation from the organising A-level Computing and IP Computing schools with strong support from the academia (NUS) and industry (FOSSASIA, Google, IMDA). Through the meaningful and engaging sessions, we hope you can learn more about the fundamentals of computer science and build sustainable learning connections with one another. Register now!
